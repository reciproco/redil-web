REDIL Get full text search on image based documents

  • Unable to extract valuable information of your documents?

    Do you have a great extent of your corporate data on scaned documents? Are you unable to classify your documents based on the information they contains?

    If you do then you have a problem. But you do not have to worry. REDIL will keep all your information accesible.

    REDIL uses OpenOCR rest API to process image based documents and extract meaningfull data. You can deploy an instance of OpenOCR in your own private cloud or use Google Compute Engine, Tutum or Orchard.

  • Are you still manual processing your digital assets with an OCR?

    Yes, you have a nice piece of software to extract the information of your documents but it does not offer you an automatic way to do it. You have to move the information around you organization between servers, filesystems ... This does not scale well. If you have an increasing amount of documents to process it becomes a real problem for you and your company.

  • REDIL will enhance your digital superpowers

    Desinged as a flexible REST API uses diferent open source compontents to provide the functionality of a OCR Server professional solution at no cost. It also ships with a powerfull and easy to use web infterface.

    The idea emerge while I was working as an IT consultant for a spanish law firm. The company had a great volume of scaned images and they longed for extract and take advantage of it. I made a research and find a lot of expensive commercial solutions but none open source.
